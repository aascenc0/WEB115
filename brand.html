Brand Guide

At Paws&Pints PUB, our objective is to provide a unique and relaxing experience that combines the joy of hanging out with your dog(s) and having a drink with some friends. We believe in creating memorable moments for our customers in a serene and delightful setting. People can enjoy a nice cold drink while their doggo runs around in the dog area with its new pals.
